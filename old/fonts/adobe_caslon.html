The Adobe Caslon font family

The Englishman William Caslon designed Caslon font. In1990 this font was released after his death. It was influenced by the characteristics of Dutch type faces. Most letter form of this font includes a cap. It is a solid, and dependable. It is an excellent text font. It contains swash letters, Old style Figures, alternates, expert characters, ligatures,central European characters & a plethora of period ornaments.
Designed 1990 by William Caslon.
